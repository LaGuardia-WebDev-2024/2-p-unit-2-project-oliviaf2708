Best places to go in Paris

Paris is beautiful, but the city
is huge, the streets can be
confusing, and you can get lost
if you don't speek french. Here
are just some places to put on
your list if you ever find youself visiting!

Le Marais

This is a neighberhood in the 4th
arrondissement, or South Marais.
There are lots of galleries,
stores, and delicious restaurants
which are perfect for a nice diner.
This neighberhood also serves as a
good area to find your hotel. There
are countless beautiful squares and
parks that are convenient to be
located near.

Avenue des Champs-Élysées

Champs-Élysées is the stret leading
up to the Arch de Triumph. There are
tons of stores to go into, ranging from
Chanel to Levi's. Along the walk, you
could also see a mix of theatres and
cafes. The famous Ladurée Bakery can
also be spotted, which have the best
macarons, in my opinion.
