Best places to go in Paris

Paris is beautiful, but the city is huge, the streets can be
confusing, and you can get lost if you don't speek french. Here
are just some places to put on your list if you ever find youself
visiting!

Le Marais

This is a neighberhood in the 4th arrondissement, or South Marais.
There are lots of galleries, stores, and delicious restaurants
which are perfect for a nice diner. This neighberhood also serves as
a good area to find your hotel. There are countless beautiful squares
and parks that are convenient to be located near.

Avenue des Champs-Élysées

Champs-Élysées is the stret leading up to the Arch de Triumph.
There are tons of stores to go into, ranging from Chanel to Levi's.
Along the walk, you could also see a mix of theatres and cafes. The
famous Ladurée Bakery can also be spotted, which have the best
macarons, in my opinion.

Dôme de Paris

The Dôme de Paris is an indoor arena located in the 15th
arrondissement. It is the largest venue in Paris, with shows
ranging from concerts and plays, to stand up comedy shows and
the ballet. Autunm and Spring are perfect times to go and see
some fantasic performances here.

La Rôtisserie d'Argent

This is a fantastic restaurant located in the Latin Quarter.
It serves upscale French cuisie and is especially famous for
thier most popular dish, the pressed duck. The food is delicious
and the service is wonderful, deffinetly a great spot to stop
during your trip.
